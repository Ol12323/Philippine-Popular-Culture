Brief Introduction of our Culture and History

Morals, ideas, and goals are expressed via culture and its history. They help to define a nation's identity. To maintain our sense of national identity, it is crucial to preserve our cultural heritage. For a group of people, culture is their entire way of life. Most people aren't even aware of how much it defines people, how they behave, and what they believe. An element of history is culture. There are numerous differences between the meanings of the terms "culture" and "history." A country or region's history focuses on its development. The interests that a certain country or land's inhabitants display are referred to as its culture.

Brief Tradition and Culture

Filipinos are influenced by the people they have interacted with in history, including Chinese, Indian, Arab and other Asian cultures. Beliefs apply every day in the life of a Filipino, revealing how rich and blessed their culture is. There are over eighty different religions in the Philippines, making it one of the most religiously diverse countries in Asia. Each religion has its own unique traditions and culture. The Catholic faith is one of the main religions in the Philippines and is practiced by approximately 84% of the population. Besides Catholicism, there are thousands of different indigenous religions and traditional beliefs that adhere to their own unique set of beliefs that are unique to their culture."
