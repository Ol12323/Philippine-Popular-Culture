People

Liza Soberano

Liza Hanley Soberano is a Filipino-American actress and model who has appeared in television series such as ‘Forevermore’ and films like ‘My Ex and Whys’. As a teenager, she started getting modeling offers. She later made her acting debut with the Philippine fantasy-drama, comedy anthology television series Wansapanataym (2011-2012). Her breakthrough came in 2014 when she starred in ABS-CBN's hit romance drama Forevermore (2014-2015)..

Jose Rizal

Jose Rizal was a polymath, however, he is best known for his nationalist writings. Rizal's work inspired the Philippine Revolution and independence from Spain. He is considered as one of the country's national heroes and his life has been depicted in several biographical films and TV series.

Ferdinand Marcos

Ferdinand Marcos served as the tenth president of the Philippines until 1986, when he was ousted in a military coup and placed under house arrest. His rule was marked by brutality, extravagance, and corruption; he was considered a kleptocrat. His dictatorship caused an economic collapse during which the country suffered greatly. He was removed from power after a series of protests called People Power Revolution in 1986.

Catriona Gray

Catriona Gray, a Filipino-Australian model who won the 2018 Miss Universe pageant, became the fourth contestant from her country to win the title. Apart from winning Miss Universe Philippines 2018, she had also been named Miss World Philippines 2016. She has worked for the OTOP initiative, too.
